OUR STORY
We created Pizzeria Vetri to celebrate the art of authentic pizza making. Simple food made exactly the right way, using the best and freshest ingredients, served in a space where all are welcome.
Come by, pull up a seat at our pizza counter and see what happens when good food, good drinks, and good people come together. The last thing the world needed was another pizzeria but maybe this one could be different. Maybe with this one, everyone could get exaclty what they wanted, made fresh on demand. It all started sounding pretty great, and soon Mr.Yuvraj opened the first PIZZERIA-VETRI in Chandigarh.
Creativity is at the heart of everything we do. No two pizzas are the same.Our menus are created by the chefs and cooks in each of our pizzerias. We are always looking for good ideas, and we are not afraid to try new things.

We're proud of what we do and how we do it. Follow @pizzeriavetri for more food facts, recipes, and menu updates!
